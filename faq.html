Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auto, home, and life insurance products.

What does auto insurance cover?
Our auto insurance covers liability, collision, comprehensive, uninsured motorists, and more. You can customize your policy to fit your needs.

How do I file a claim?
You can file a claim online, by phone, or through our mobile app. Our team will guide you through every step.

Can I add multiple vehicles to my policy?
Yes! We offer multi-vehicle discounts and make it easy to manage all your cars under one policy.

Do you offer roadside assistance?
Yes, roadside assistance is available as an add-on to your policy for towing, flat tires, and more.

How can I lower my auto insurance premium?
Safe driving, bundling policies, and maintaining a good credit score can help reduce your premium.

What types of homes do you insure?
We cover houses, condos, apartments, and rental properties. Flexible plans are available for owners and renters.

Does home insurance cover natural disasters?
Standard policies cover many risks, but you can add extra protection for floods, earthquakes, and more.

How do I update my coverage?
Contact our support team or log in to your account to make changes to your policy at any time.

What is not covered by home insurance?
Typical exclusions include normal wear and tear, pest damage, and certain natural disasters unless you add extra coverage.

Can I get a discount for home security systems?
Yes, installing security systems or smoke detectors may qualify you for discounts.

What types of life insurance do you offer?
We offer term life, whole life, and universal life insurance. Our advisors can help you choose the best option.

Do I need a medical exam?
Some policies require a medical exam, but we also offer no-exam options for qualified applicants.

How much coverage do I need?
Coverage needs vary by person. We recommend considering your income, debts, and family needs. Our online tools can help you estimate the right amount.

Can I change my beneficiaries?
Yes, you can update your beneficiaries at any time by contacting us or through your online account.

What happens if I miss a payment?
Most policies have a grace period. Contact us as soon as possible to avoid a lapse in coverage.
